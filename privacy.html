Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Novlyn builds simple, private iOS apps. This policy covers HabitJournal and MedJournal.

Data We Collect

Our apps store data you enter (habits, medication logs, etc.) locally on your device and, where enabled, in your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. We do not have access to this data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not run ads

HabitJournal

All habit data is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account (if iCloud is enabled). No data leaves your devices.

MedJournal

Medication logs are stored locally and synced via your personal iCloud account. Caregiver notifications are delivered using Apple Push Notification service (APNs) through CloudKit — no third-party servers are involved.
