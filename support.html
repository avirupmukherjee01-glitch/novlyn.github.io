HabitJournal

  • Streaks not updating? Make sure the habit frequency matches your intended schedule.
  • Rest day not working? Rest days can only be applied to today or future dates, up to 3 per month.
  • Notifications not arriving? Check Settings → Notifications → HabitJournal and ensure notifications are allowed.

MedJournal

  • Caregiver not receiving notifications? Both devices must be signed into iCloud and have notifications enabled for MedJournal.
  • Medicine not showing? Try closing and reopening the app to refresh the list.
  • Wrong time showing for a dose? Check that both devices are set to the correct time zone.

General

  • iCloud sync not working? Go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud and make sure the app is enabled.
  • Want to request a feature? Email us — we read every message.
